GeoTicker

GeoTicker is a real-time visualization of earthquakes as they are reported by various authorities through the world. It was created by Jay Langford. You can reach out at geoticker@jaylangford.net.

Real-time earthquake data are provided by the Euro-Mediterranean Seismological Centre through their SeismicPortal service. These data are under the CC BY 4.0 license. Thanks to the European Plate Observing System for their DataPortal service, which was useful for data discovery during this project.

Geographic data are from Natural Earth, made available in GeoJSON format by geojson.xyz.
